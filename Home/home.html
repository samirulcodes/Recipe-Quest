Pancakes

Put the flour, eggs, milk, 1 tbsp oil and a pinch of salt into a bowl or large jug, then whisk to a smooth batter. Set aside for 30 mins to rest if you have time, or start cooking straight away. Set a medium frying pan or crêpe pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper. When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden, keeping them warm in a low oven as you go.

Madeira Cake

Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 160°C (320°F) and grease and line a 7-inch round cake tin.Cream Butter and Sugar: In a bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Add Zest and Fold in the lemon zest and flour alternately with the milk until well combined.Bake: Pour the mixture into the prepared tin and smooth the top. Bake in the preheated oven for 60-70 minutes or until a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.Allow the cake to cool in the tin for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to coolcompletely.

Chicken Biryani

Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala, red chili powder). Let it sit for 30 minutes.Cook Rice: Boil basmati rice until 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.Prepare Masala: Sauté onions until golden. Add tomatoes, marinated chicken, and cook until chicken is done.Layer: In a pot, layer half the rice, then the chicken masala, then the remaining rice. Top with saffron milk, fried onions, and fresh herbs (mint, cilantro).Cover the pot with a tight lid. Cook on low heat (dum) for 20-25 minutes and then serve

Chicken Butter Masala

Rinse & Soak: Rinse 1 cup basmati rice and soak for 20 minutes.Heat Oil: In a pan, heat 2 tbsp oil or ghee.Add Spices: Add 1 tsp cumin seeds and let them splutter.Sauté: Add 1 chopped green chili and sauté for a minute. Cook Rice: Drain rice, add to the pan with 2 cups water, and salt to taste. Simmer: Bring to a boil, then simmer on low heat until rice is cooked (about 15 minutes).Fluff & Serve: Fluff with a fork and serve hot.
